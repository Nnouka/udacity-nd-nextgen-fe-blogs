Burna boy in Paris

With his combination of dancehall, reggae, afrobeat and pop, Burna Boy emerged in the early 2010s as one of Nigeria's most promising personalities.

The 2012 LeriQ-produced single "Like to Party" proved to be her signature track and paved the way for her full-length debut, L.I.F.E, a year later. Over the next five years, Burna released two more albums and collaborated with a variety of artists, from Skales to Fall Out Boy to Lilly Allen.
